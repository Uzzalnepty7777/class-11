Bodybuilding is a sport focused on developing and enhancing the body's
muscles through a rigorous training regimen that combines weightlifting,
cardiovascular exercise, and strict nutrition, aiming to achieve a
visually aesthetic physique with pronounced muscle definition, symmetry,
and size; unlike other strength sports like powerlifting, bodybuilding
prioritizes aesthetics over maximum weight lifted, with competitions
judging competitors based on their overall muscular presentation and
posing on stage.
